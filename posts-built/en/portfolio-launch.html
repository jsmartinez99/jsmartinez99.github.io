Portfolio Launch

Portfolio Live

After years of recommending that clients maintain a professional online presence, I finally practice what I preach. This portfolio was built with a clear goal: centralize my professional trajectory in one accessible, fast, and secure place.

Tech Stack

  • Static HTML/CSS — no frameworks, no unnecessary JavaScript
  • Served with nginx — reverse proxy with self-signed TLS
  • Orchestrated with opencode — 7 specialized AI agents following the SDD flow
  • EN/ES Internationalization — language toggle with automatic detection
